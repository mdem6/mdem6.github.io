Course Overview

DATA 352W challenges us to think critically about the ethical, social, and technical dimensions of data science. The course addresses:

  • Ethical Challenges: We delve into labor policies, hiring practices, and workplace non-discrimination, assessing the ethical use of technologies.
  • Global Impact of Tech Monopolies: Exploring the dominance of tech giants and their effects on a global scale.
  • Open Source Contributions: Engaging with platforms like GitHub to understand the value of open datasets and collaborative innovation.
  • Socially Responsible Research: Emphasizing research that positively impacts society and promotes social responsibility.
  • Technology Accessibility: Ensuring that advances in technology are accessible to everyone, fostering an inclusive environment.
